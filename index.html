Selected

Advertising Works

Greatness In Legacy

OTP Bank

In this image campaign/short movie, I took the “Meant for Greatness” claim of the brand, and dissected it with a cultural lens. Inspired by “The Cloud Atlass” the commercial treats humanity’s calling for greatness and remembrance through the ages. The values that we carry today and will inherit to the future generations are passed along through millenia by storytellers of all sorts.

End of Year Jingle

Big Market

In this musical-styled ad, I wrote the lyrics to the song, which turned into an infectious viral sensation. Everyone and they mother knew the lyrics to the song. This is reflected in Big Market’s YouTube channel, where the jingle is the most viewed video by a huge difference. (It has 275,000 views as compared to the second most viewed video, which has only 14,000)

Bite-sized Love

KMY

This ad for mini-saussages utilizes puppets and whimsical, cartoonish comedy to aim at it’s target audience: children. It’s wholesome, funny, and somewhat surreal at times and is by far the most original and memorable commercial of the meat-processing brand.
